## Gamma Prompt

> Create a 5-slide pitch deck for "VisionCheck AI" - a mobile-first eye health screening app. Use a modern healthcare aesthetic with sky blue (#0ea5e9) and slate colors. Professional, investor-ready style.
>
> **Slide 1 - The Problem & Market (25% weight - Product Viability):**
> Title: "2.2 Billion People Have Vision Problems—Most Preventable." The problem: Eye exams cost $200+ and require specialist appointments. 50% of vision loss is preventable with early detection. Underserved communities lack access to basic screening. The market: $18B global vision screening market. 4.2B smartphone users worldwide. Growing telehealth adoption post-pandemic. This is a real product solving a real problem for real users.
>
> **Slide 2 - AI Innovation (25% weight - Technical Innovation):**
> Title: "GPT-4o Vision + 7 Clinical Screening Tests." Key AI features: 1) GPT-4o Vision API analyzes eye photos for redness, cloudiness, pupil abnormalities, and visible health indicators. 2) Context-aware AI assistant that interprets your personal test results and answers follow-up questions. 3) Voice commands via Web Speech API for hands-free operation during tests. Novel approach: Combines traditional ophthalmology screening methods (Ishihara, Pelli-Robson, Amsler Grid, Tumbling E) with modern AI analysis in a single integrated platform.
>
> **Slide 3 - Working Demo (20% weight - Execution):**
> Title: "Fully Functional. Fully Tested. Ship-Ready." Complete feature set: Visual Acuity (Tumbling E), Color Vision (Ishihara plates), Contrast Sensitivity (Pelli-Robson), Amsler Grid (macular degeneration), Astigmatism (clock dial), Peripheral Vision (glaucoma screening), AI Eye Photo Analysis, Health Snapshot dashboard with history tracking. Quality: Comprehensive E2E test suite (Playwright), unit tests (Vitest), real-time error boundaries. Tech stack: React 19, Vite, Tailwind CSS 4, Node.js API proxy for secure AI calls.
>
> **Slide 4 - User Experience (15% weight - UX & Design):**
> Title: "Accessible by Design." Highlight: Dark/light mode, multi-language (EN/DE), voice commands, audio instructions for each test, consent management, GDPR-ready privacy controls, PWA-installable. User flow: Onboarding → Select test → Clear instructions with audio → Perform test → Instant results with AI interpretation → Health Snapshot summary → Share/export results. Show mobile screenshots demonstrating polished UI, intuitive navigation, and accessibility features.
>
> **Slide 5 - Vision & Call to Action (15% weight - Presentation):**
> Title: "Making Eye Health Accessible to Everyone." Impact: Enable early detection before conditions become severe. Reduce barriers for underserved communities. Empower users to track their eye health over time. Bridge the gap between annual checkups. Next steps: Try the live demo. Note: Educational screening tool—always consult professionals for diagnosis. End with app logo and tagline: "VisionCheck AI - See Your Health Clearly."

## Alternative: Simple Version (Single Prompt)

If you prefer a simpler approach, use this single prompt:

> Create a 5-slide pitch deck for "VisionCheck AI" - a mobile-first web app that democratizes eye health screening using AI. Use a modern, clean healthcare aesthetic with a sky blue (#0ea5e9) and slate color palette. Style: professional yet approachable.
>
> **Slide 1 - Title/Hook:**
> VisionCheck AI - Mobile Eye Health Pre-Screening. Tagline: "Professional-grade vision tests in your pocket." Show the concept of a smartphone being used for eye care. Include a striking statistic: 2.2 billion people worldwide have vision impairment, and over 1 billion cases could have been prevented with early screening.
>
> **Slide 2 - The Problem:**
> Eye care is inaccessible for many. Key pain points: 1) Eye exams require expensive appointments and travel to specialists. 2) Early signs of conditions like macular degeneration, glaucoma, and color blindness often go undetected. 3) No convenient way to track vision health over time. 4) Existing apps are single-purpose and lack AI intelligence.
>
> **Slide 3 - The Solution:**
> VisionCheck AI offers 7 comprehensive vision tests: Visual Acuity (Tumbling E), Color Vision (Ishihara plates), Contrast Sensitivity (Pelli-Robson), Amsler Grid (macular degeneration), Astigmatism Screening (clock dial), Peripheral Vision (glaucoma), and AI Eye Photo Analysis powered by GPT-4o Vision. Plus: an AI chat assistant that answers eye health questions, personalized health snapshots, progress tracking, voice commands for accessibility, and multi-language support.
>
> **Slide 4 - Technical Innovation:**
> Built with React 19, Vite, and Tailwind CSS 4. Key innovations: GPT-4o Vision API analyzes eye photos for visible health indicators, AI assistant provides personalized guidance based on test results, full accessibility (voice commands, audio instructions, dark mode), privacy-first design with consent management, PWA-ready for offline use, comprehensive test suite with Playwright E2E and Vitest unit tests.
>
> **Slide 5 - Impact & Call to Action:**
> Vision: Make preliminary eye health screening accessible to everyone, anywhere. Potential impact: Enable early detection of vision issues before they become severe, reduce barriers to eye care in underserved communities, empower users to take control of their eye health. Call to action: Try the demo now. Note: Educational screening tool - always consult professionals for diagnosis.

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Upload screenshots** - After generating, replace placeholder images with actual app screenshots
2. **Add the demo video** - Link to or embed the demo video for judges
3. **Include live demo URL** - Add a QR code or link to the deployed app
4. **Keep text minimal** - Let visuals tell the story; use speaker notes for details
